About Me

As a native to Nicaragua, I was given a unigue perspective when I moved to the United States in 2004. It taught me to appreciate the little things, which is a practice I do to this day. Living in Maryland, and later living in Maine; taught me the differences between living in large and small populations.

I pride myself on having a very open mind. My goal in life is to grow every aspect of myself as much as possible. I believe when people settle and stop pushing forward is when their dreams start to fade. This is why most people never achieve what they want to achieve in life. I will not be one of those people.
